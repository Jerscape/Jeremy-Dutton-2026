About

I have taken a slightly unusual path: HR first, then workforce development and social services, and then tech. I now offer both sides of that experience, sometimes together and sometimes separately, depending on the goal.

I like work that is practical and useful. I help clarify what matters, build buy-in, and move projects forward through facilitation, project management, training and course development, CRM support, and problem solving.

I am passionate and committed in all that I do. If you have a problem you think I can solve, or a contribution I can make, let's talk!
